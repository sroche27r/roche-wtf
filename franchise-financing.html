Franchise Financing

IGH Funding Franchise Division is specifically focused on the franchised restaurant industry. Our expertise is understanding our customers’ and prospects’ business.

How IGH Funding Can Help

IGH Funding Franchise Finance provides a full array of small business loan products to multi-unit franchisees and franchisors of national and regional restaurant brands. Through the IGH Funding approach, we tailor restaurant loans to the needs of the respective franchisee or franchisor with an emphasis on strong concepts, solid cash flow coverage, appropriate capital structure, and an attractive collateral mix between fee simple, space-leased, and ground-leased restaurant units.

We offer 100% financing for all types of equipment, fixed payment schedules linked to business seasons and cycles, streamlined approvals, and the convenient option to add new equipment to existing leases.

Solutions Offerings:

  • Business Value / Cash Flow Financing
  • Equipment
  • Store Purchases / Acquisitions
  • Remodeling
  • Refinance
  • Capital Loans
  • SBA Loans
  • Recapitalization
