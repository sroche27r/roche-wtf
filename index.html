WORKING CAPITAL LOAN

Typically, these loans are unsecured and are used to cover operating expenses while business funds are tied up elsewhere. These loans require a minimum of 6 months in business, a business bank account, a minimum of deposits, and revenue coming into the business and a passing personal credit score of the owner.
